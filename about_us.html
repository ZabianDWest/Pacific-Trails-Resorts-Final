About Us

Pacific Trails Resort is a peaceful coastal destination along California’s North Coast, featuring beautiful ocean views, serene landscapes, and outdoor experiences that connect guests with nature.

Guests can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking nearby trails, kayaking along the coastline, and observing local wildlife, all while taking in the quiet and relaxing natural environment.

Pacific Trails Resort represents a balance between comfort and adventure, offering visitors a unique escape that encourages relaxation, exploration, and a deeper connection with the natural beauty of the coast.
